The calculator that should exist.

Every programmer needs to convert between hex and decimal. Check a bitmask. Do quick bitwise math. macOS Calculator has a programmer mode buried in a menu, and it's clunky.


Hexa lives in your menu bar. Click it, type a value, see all representations instantly. Type expressions like 0xdead + 0xbeef and watch it evaluate live. Native Swift, native SwiftUI, opens instantly.
